We Love Each Other

Our Story

  • Bethlehem, PA

    It was Fall 2011. Shijue has just settled down in the holy town of Bethlehem, and started school at Lehigh. Haosu and his dorm mates decided to organize a hotpot/game night and invited a couple of friends over. Dinner and board games with friends were catalysts for some miracle between the two. They became friends after that and started to learn more about each other. They found out that they share a lot in common; they enjoy the same genres of books, movies and music.

  • Parsippany, NJ & Cambridge, MA

    2015

    Time flies and all of a sudden Shijue and Haosu had to part. Shijue was starting her first job in New Jersey and Haosu was moving to Massachusetts. Love didn't fade with long distance but rather bonded them closer together.

  • New York, NY & Austin, TX

    2017

    Since Haosu moved to New York City, Shijue had to commute 3 hours a day for work so they can live together. Even this didn't last long as Haosu had to relocate to Austin. They decided to face this second parting with optimism and is hopeful for the future. Seeing the sacrifice for each other, realizing they are partners in times of joy and sorrow, they decided to tie the knot.
